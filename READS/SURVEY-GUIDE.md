## New Survey Questions (1-5 Scale)

### 1. Greek Life Involvement
**Question:** "How involved are you in Greek life?"
- **1** = Not involved at all
- **5** = Very active member

**Why it matters:** Matches students with similar social structures and commitments.

### 2. Club/Organization Involvement  
**Question:** "How many clubs/organizations are you actively involved in?"
- **1** = 0-1 clubs
- **5** = 5+ clubs

**Why it matters:** Indicates time commitment and organizational experience.

### 3. Nightlife Participation
**Question:** "How often do you participate in nightlife activities?"
- **1** = Rarely/Never
- **5** = Multiple times a week

**Why it matters:** Helps match lifestyle compatibility and social habits.

### 4. Academic Focus/Priority
**Question:** "How would you rate your academic focus/priority?"
- **1** = Balanced/Social focus
- **5** = Academics first priority

**Why it matters:** Core to the mentorship relationship and career guidance.

### 5. Study Habits
**Question:** "What are your typical study habits?"
- **1** = Study in groups/socially
- **5** = Study alone/quietly

**Why it matters:** Indicates work style and approach to learning.
